Rainy Days

PRESENTATION

I had to make a properly functioning responsive website for an online webshop selling men's and womens rain jackets. The target audience is men and women aged 30-50 years, whose interests are hiking, skiing, exploring, camping and cannoeing.

This was my first website i made and we were only allowed to use HTM & CSS.

SELF EVALUATION

If i re-created this website, i would have changed a couple of things. First thing first, i would have resized the logo and placed it on the left corner. I would have changed the navigation bar menu to another colour and placed the search-bar icon along with the search field. The headings and pergraphs should be aligned left instead of center alignment.

I would have changed the size of the pictures on every page and placed them correctly. The footer is also very dark and i would have changed it to a slightly lighter colour.

At the "about us" page i would definately kept the peragraphs shorter in width.

PROCESS

For this site i was only allowed to use HTML & CSS. It was my first website and the knowledge i had was very limited. Through out the process i watched a couple of videoes and tutorias to get some ideas, for example, "how to make a responsive and fine contact form".

Even though it was my first website and two months into this course, i remember how satisfied i felt when i finished this project.
